Hello!

I'm Chanidu Senarathna, an aspiring software engineer and machine learning enthusiast from Bandarawela, Sri Lanka. With a deep passion for technology and a strong self-taught foundation, I’ve spent the last four years exploring the intersections of software development, artificial intelligence, and data science.

While I hold a Diploma in Information Technology, most of my expertise comes from self-driven learning and real-world projects. From building backend systems using frameworks like .NET and Django to working with machine learning models in areas like Natural Language Processing (NLP) and Computer Vision (CV), I’ve been steadily expanding both my technical and problem-solving skills.

My journey began long before the classroom. Whether I’m developing intelligent systems, analyzing data, or diving into the latest developments in AI and large language models, I’m driven by a genuine excitement to learn and create.

I’m always looking for opportunities to collaborate, grow, and contribute meaningfully to forward-thinking projects. Thanks for stopping by my portfolio — I hope it gives you a glimpse into my passion and potential.
